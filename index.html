Fight Club (1999)

2h 16m
8.8

"Fight Club" tells the story of Jack, an ordinary office worker who is disillusioned with life. Jack suffers from insomnia and is living under the influence of television commercials. Seeking to add some excitement to his life, Jack attends a support group, where he meets Marla Singer. Later, he encounters the charismatic and rebellious Tyler Durden. Tyler introduces Jack to a new perspective on masculinity and freedom, and they together establish "Fight Club," a secret and violent underground club.

However, the success of the club and Tyler's radical philosophy begin to threaten Jack's life and mental stability. The film delves into themes of consumerism and masculinity, offering a thrilling and thought-provoking narrative.
